About Me

Hey there! My name is Thomas Smith and I'm a web developer/artist living in RVA. I'm very interested in design work, how to structure web pages in interesting and creative ways, and where I can include my art in the design process itself.

I've lived in Richmond all of my life, except a brief 7 months that I spent in Stuttgart, Germany during highschool where I attended the Fanny-Leicht Gymnasium. Like they say in Germany:

"Wenn's dem Esel zu wohl ist, geht er aufs Eis tanzen, und ich bin der Esel."

When I'm not coding I listen to music until my ears bleed in reasonable moderation, play Dungeons and Dragons on the weekends, draw, create jewelry, and await inevitable reign of our lizard overlords.
